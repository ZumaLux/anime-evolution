CMFU Xueyuan: Wangzi Peng Peng Qiu (Prince of Billiard)

The young Qin Xialolu was raised by her grandmother since her paretnts divorced when she was only a child. To make a living, althought she was still a young girl, she was forced to leave school and start work, where she learned how to play pool and do tricks.
One day, as she was running away from the trouble she was into, the young girl met a guy, an owner of a BMV, and tried to take advantage of him as well. But things did not go as she expected...
