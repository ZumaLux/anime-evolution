FGO Camelot Movie and Babylonia Anime Release Visual and PV!

AnimeJapan 2019 saw plenty of exciting news for Fate fans, with movie Fate/Grand Order: Shinsei Entaku Ryouiki Camelot revealing a concept visual and its 2020 premiere, as well as TV anime Fate/Grand Order: Zettai Maju Sensen Babylonia releasing its newest trailer!

The movie visual features a striking depiction of Bedivere, who will be voiced by Miyano Mamoru. Other confirmed cast members include Shimazaki Nobunaga as Fujimaru Ritsuka, and Takahashi Rie as Mash Kyrielight.

Suezawa Kei will direct the first part of the two-part movie ( Wandering; Agateram ) with Arai Kazuto handling the second ( Paladin; Agateram ). Haga Keita and Fukasawa Hideyuki will be handling the music with Hosoi Mieko providing character design, Production I.G. producing and, and SIGNAL.MD contributing to animation production on the first film. Production I.G. will also be leading animation production for the second part.

A new key visual and PV have also been released for the upcoming TV anime Fate/Grand Order: Zettai Maju Sensen Babylonia, which will adapt the Babylonia arc of the popular smartphone game.

Fate/Grand Order: Zettai Maju Sensen Babylonia will air in Oct. 2019, while Fate/Grand Order: Shinsei Entaku Ryouiki Camelot will premiere sometime in 2020.
