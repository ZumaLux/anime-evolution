The Promised Neverland Season 2 Set for 2020!

Popular anime The Promised Neverland will be back for a second season next year.

Inspired by the manga written by Shirai Kaiu and illustrated by Demizu Posuka, the series follows orphans Emma and Norman who live with 36 other children at Grace Field House as they discover a terrible secret after venturing somewhere they were not supposed to go.

The Promised Neverland Season 2 will air in 2020.
