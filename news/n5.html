The Seven Deadly Sins Gets New Anime Season For Autumn 2019!

A new anime series for The Seven Deadly Sins is set to air in Autumn 2019 under the title The Seven Deadly Sins: Kamigami no Gekirin!

While details are still scarce, the new series promises fans plenty of action as it heads to the story's climax, including clashes against the Ten Commandments, more developments in Meliodas and Elizabeth's connection, and the secrets of the world of The Seven Deadly Sins.

Inspired by the popular manga by Suzuki Nanaba, Season 1 of the TV anime adaptation aired in 2014 and was followed by an original story TV special. It also received a second season and movie in 2018.

The Seven Deadly Sins: Kamigami no Gekirin will air in Autumn 2019.
